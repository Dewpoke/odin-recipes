A flat pancake by Zienab Mokhtar

This pancake recipe by GERAL1012 is something quick for when you really want pancakes but don't want complicated.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flower
  • 1 and 1/2 tablespoons of baking powder

Steps

  1. Place milk, sugar, eggs, oil and vanilla in the blender. Add flour and baking powder. Blend until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
