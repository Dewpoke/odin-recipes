Omelette recipe

An omelette by Soup Loving Nicole

A quick and simple cheese omelette recipe, by topfgucker. Eggs, milk, butter, cheese, and salt and pepper are all that's needed!

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of milk, or as needed
  • salt and freshly ground white pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (Pick your favourite!)
