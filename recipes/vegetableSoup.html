Vegetable soup by Buckwheat Queen

This vegetable soup recipe from Adam Ragusea follows the age old technique of taking whatever ingredients you have, throwing them in boiling water, and then eating it.

Ingredients

  • Pot of water
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Any vegetable. Maybe something green to make it look nice.
  • Well any vegetable you think is good
  • White wine
  • Frozen peas
  • Clove of garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Basic seasoning (or whater you like)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot. Protip: start with less water rather than too much as it is easy to add more water, but hard to take out water.
  2. Cut carrots and onions and throw them in the pot, as well as other 'hard' vegetables you have choses such as potatoes.
  3. Stir the pot occasionally. Repeat this step whenever you feel like stirring the pot.
  4. Cut tomatoes into random chunks and toss them in the pot.
  5. Do the same with your other vegetables.
  6. Pour in a bunch of wine.
  7. Turn off the stove when you are happy with the softness of the soup, or are hungry.
  8. Throw in frozen peas to add greens and help cool off the soup.
  9. Crush the clove of garlic and toss it in the pot.
  10. Place soup in bowl and add a dash of olive oil and seasoning before serving.
